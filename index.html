X-Meng Group: Physics X Network

The physics of network, or the network of physics?

This rhetorical question captures the interdisciplinary essence of the X-Meng Group’s mission — to explore the rich interplay between network science and both statistical and quantum physics.

Led by Professor Xiangyi Meng, the group’s research spans several areas:

  • Quantum networks, communications, and computing.
  • Physical/biological networks (and their connections to string theory).
  • Statistical physics of complex networks, network metrics.
  • Open quantum systems, open quantum field theories.
